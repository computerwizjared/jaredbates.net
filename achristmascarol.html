A Christmas Carol Webquest

Introduction

In this web quest, you will discover and write about the four themes of A Christmas Carol.

Task at Hand

For each of the ghosts (Ghost of Christmas Past, Present, and Yet-To-Come), you will write a paragraph describing the themes that you find, and how it relates to you or someone you know.

Process

  1. First, you must read A Christmas Carol fully. (I suggest reading it a second time so that you gain a better understanding of the story.)
  2. Second, find and take note of the themes emphasized in each of the three staves.
  3. Third, write a paragraph for each of the themes found in the story. Be sure to include quotes and references from the story for each theme.
  4. If you'd like, you may trade paragraphs with a friend and compare the themes you found, and fix them together.

Resources

  • A Christmas Carol
  • If you get stuck, look here for ideas. You must still have references to the story itself.

Evaluation

Ensure that you have written about at least four themes. There may be more, but there are four which are essential to the story.

Conclusion

By the end of this webquest, you should have an in-depth understanding of the four essential themes of A Christmas Carol.
